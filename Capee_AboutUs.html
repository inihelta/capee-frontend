WHO ARE WE?

Founded in Banjarbaru, CAPEE was created with a passion for bringing people together through great coffee, warm conversations, and a comfortable atmosphere. Designed as a modern lifestyle café, CAPEE is more than just a place to enjoy drinks it is a space where people can relax, work, and create meaningful moments every day.

With a cozy and modern ambiance combined with friendly service, CAPEE is committed to delivering a unique experience for every customer. We believe that exceptional coffee begins with premium-quality ingredients and carefully crafted recipes made with dedication and consistency..

Beyond coffee, CAPEE also offers a variety of delicious meals and light bites that perfectly complement every moment. From casual hangouts with friends to productive meetings and family gatherings, our menu is designed to suit every occasion and lifestyle.

As trends and lifestyles continue to evolve, CAPEE continuously adapts and innovates to provide the best experience for our customers. Carrying the spirit of #TrueCoffeeCompanion and #SharingMoments, CAPEE continues its journey from Banjarbaru with the vision of expanding into more cities and becoming a favorite destination for coffee lovers everywhere.
